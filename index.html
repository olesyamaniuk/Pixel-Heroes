Tales of Emond

Forge Your Legend, Become a Hero!

GAME DESCRIPTION

Pixel Heroes: Tales of Emond is a tactical RPG where you assemble a team of legendary heroes and lead them into battles against dark forces threatening the kingdom of Emond. The game blends an engaging storyline, deep character progression, and strategic turn-based combat.
Discover new heroes, collect powerful gear, explore mysterious lands, and make choices that shape the fate of the world!
